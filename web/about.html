Our Story

CampusKart was born out of a simple observation: college students often struggle to find quality products at affordable prices. Founded in 2023, we set out to create a platform that would make campus life easier and more enjoyable.

What started as a small online store has grown into a comprehensive marketplace, serving thousands of students across multiple campuses. Our journey has been guided by one simple principle: to make student life better, one product at a time.

Our Mission

At CampusKart, our mission is to empower students by providing:

  • High-quality products at student-friendly prices
  • A seamless shopping experience
  • Reliable delivery to campus locations
  • Excellent customer service
